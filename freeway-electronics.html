Founded in Limassol in 2006, Freeway Electronics Ltd. has grown from a small import business into Cyprus's most trusted electronics distributor. We partner with the world's top manufacturers to bring certified, warranty-backed technology to consumers and enterprises across the island and the wider Eastern Mediterranean region.

Every product we carry has been rigorously tested and meets EU standards for safety and performance.
